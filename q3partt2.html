instruction

First, slice your bread loaf in ½ inches slices. You want about 8 to 10 slices total, depending on how many cheese sandwiches you are making. Here I have used homemade Whole Wheat Bread. Top half of the slices with 3 to 4 tablespoons or more (depending on the size of the bread slices) of freshly grated cheddar or mozzarella cheese, or a mix of both. For 8 to 10 slices you will need a total of about 1 cup of grated cheese. Sprinkle with a pinch or two of various dried herbs, red chilli flakes and crushed black pepper. Here I’ve used parsley, thyme and oregano with bits of chilli pepper flakes. Feel free to add your favorite herbs. Remove the grilled sandwich and transfer to a chopping board. Cut in two and serve hot with Ketchup, Mayo or any dip of your choice. Make all the cheese sandwiches this way on the skillet. If you use a large skillet, grill two to three sandwiches at the same time on the skillet.
